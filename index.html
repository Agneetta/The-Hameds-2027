Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

We kindly request semi-formal attire for our celebration. For men, this means a suit and tie. For women, a cocktail dress, an elegant slip dress, or a formal jumpsuit.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we are only able to accommodate the guests formally named on your invitation envelope. If your invitation says "and Guest," you are welcome to bring a plus one.

Are children invited?

While we adore your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening.

Will the events be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will take place outdoors (weather permitting), and the reception will immediately follow indoors.
